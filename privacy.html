Chef Mimi — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 July 2026

Chef Mimi ("the app," "we," "us") is a voice-first cooking assistant for iPhone. This policy explains what data the app handles and why. We built the app to collect as little as possible.

No account, no personal details

Chef Mimi has no sign-up or login. We do not ask for or collect your name, email address, phone number, contacts, location, photos, or any other personal identifying information.

Microphone and speech

When you hold the button to talk, the app listens through your microphone so it can understand your cooking questions. On supported iPhones this speech-to-text conversion happens on your device using Apple's speech recognition. Your microphone audio is not recorded, stored, or uploaded by us. Only the resulting text of what you asked is sent onward so the assistant can reply.

What is sent to our server

To answer you, the text of your request is sent over an encrypted (HTTPS) connection to our server (hosted on Google Cloud), which relays it to our AI provider, DeepSeek, to generate a cooking response. The reply is sent back to your phone and spoken aloud by an on-device voice. We do not use this content to build a profile of you, and we do not sell it.

Anonymous device identifier

The app generates a random identifier the first time it runs and includes it with requests to our server. Its only purpose is to prevent abuse and apply fair-use rate limits. It is not linked to your Apple ID or your identity, and it is discarded if you delete and reinstall the app.

Data stored on your device

Recipes, your cooking history, and app settings (such as your chosen voice) are stored only on your iPhone. They are not uploaded to us. Deleting the app removes them.

No tracking, no ads, no analytics

Chef Mimi contains no advertising, no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs, and does not track you across other apps or websites.

Third parties who process data on our behalf

  • Google Cloud — hosts the server that relays your requests.
  • DeepSeek — the AI service that generates cooking responses from your text.

These providers process the text of your requests solely to deliver the app's functionality.

Children

Chef Mimi is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the date at the top of this page.
