Chef Mimi — Support

Chef Mimi is a hands-free, voice-first cooking assistant. Hold the button, ask what you want to cook, and she talks you through it step by step.

Getting started

  • Allow microphone and speech recognition when first asked — Mimi needs them to hear you.
  • Speak in English, then pause; Mimi replies when you finish talking.
  • An internet connection is required so Mimi can think of recipes and answers.

Common questions

Mimi isn't responding. Check that you have an internet connection and that microphone + speech access are enabled in iOS Settings → Chef Mimi.

She can't hear me. Reduce background noise and speak toward the phone. Mimi listens in English only.
